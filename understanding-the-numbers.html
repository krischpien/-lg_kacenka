Understanding the Numbers

Multi-channel Sound Bar systems use multiple speakers to produce sound that surrounds you and manufacturers have developed a simple two-digit shorthand to explain the number of speaker outputs or ‘channels’ included, for example, ‘2.1’ or ‘5.1’. The first number details the number of full-range speaker channels horizontally that are capable of delivering sounds over the full audio spectrum. The second number indicates the number of special ‘low frequency effects’ sub-woofer outputs (these provide the floor-shaking sounds from explosions and kick-drums).
